Installation and service of paternosters

«RETAIL SERVICE» company has extensive experience in assembling and installation of paternosters of different producers: A5 Creation, Technovendor, IPRIS etc.

Service of paternosters includes:

  • inspection of the retaining elements of paternoster;
  • chain tension control and adjustment;
  • cleaning and greasing of chain;
  • inspection and selective replacement of fixators on the chain;
  • adjustment of the upper shaft tension;
  • electric motor contacts inspection, cleaning and reconnection if needed;
  • replacement of the duct tape on the shafts.
